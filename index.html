Alex Cunningham is a Central Coast native — raised by the rhythm of the ocean, shaped by the mountains of Tahoe, and now rooted back home in San Luis Obispo where her passion for skin officially became her life's work.

After graduating from the Design School of Cosmetology, Alex earned her esthetician license and went on to become a certified Face Reality Acne Specialist — one of the most respected acne training programs in the industry.

But the path to skincare wasn't just professional. It was personal.

Like so many of her clients, Alex struggled with acne as a teenager. She knows firsthand how a breakout isn't just a skin issue — it's a confidence issue. It changes how you show up in a room, how you look in photos, how you feel walking out the door in the morning. That lived experience is what drives everything at Steady Glow.

When Alex works with a client on acne, she's not just treating skin. She's treating the whole person behind it.

Steady Glow Esthetics exists because Alex wanted to create the resource she wished she'd had — a knowledgeable, caring partner who could cut through the noise, explain what's actually happening in your skin, and build a real plan toward lasting clarity.
